Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the material of the t-shirts?

Our spirituality-themed t-shirts are made from high-quality, 100% polyester jersey that is comfortable to wear and easy to care for. They are made in the US, and are slim-fit.

2. How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email that you can use to track your order. It will also give you an estimate of how long it will take for your order to be delivered. Please see the Track my Order page for more information.

3. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Please see the Shipping Time Frames page for more information on international shipping rates and delivery times.

4. What is your return policy?

We have a hassle-free return policy that allows you to return any item within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Please see the Returns & Exchanges page for more information.

5. How do I care for my t-shirt?

To ensure that your t-shirt lasts for a long time, we recommend washing it in cold water and tumble drying it on low heat.

6. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, as well as PayPal, afterpay and Google Pay.

7. How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team through the Customer Support page. We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
